I've always enjoyed music discovery services. When a new service is launched, I've been known to spend hours playing around seeing what new music the service can find for me. So I figured it would be fun to build my own music / artist discovery service. I call it Markov Music.

When you type something into the search bar, we try and match this search term to a known person. Using the Freebase database, we then look for other artists that influenced and were influenced by the artist you entered. After showing you a YouTube video of this artist, we'll randomly select another artist from the list of influenced and influenced by. And so forth and so on. Hence the name Markov Music - the artist selection technique forms a Markov Chain.
